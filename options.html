Keybindings

All keybindings should be specified in Keymaster format. Examples:
  • a
  • ctrl+a
  • command+a
  • a, ctrl+b, command+c - multiple shortcuts that will be treated equivalently
Special keys names: backspace, tab, clear, enter, return, esc, escape, space, up, down, left, right, home, end, pageup, pagedown, del, delete, and f1 through f19
